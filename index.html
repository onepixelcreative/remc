What we do

Estimating, end-to-end.

From document triage to a qualified, submission-ready bid — REMC handles the full preconstruction workflow for UK electrical contractors.

Take-off & BOQ

Bluebeam-marked drawings audited against the issue revision, then translated into a clean, sectioned Bill of Quantities you can defend in any post-tender review.

Sub & material enquiries

Coordinated enquiry packs to your preferred suppliers and subcontractors. We track responses, normalise quotes, and feed the lowest credible rate into your tender.

Submission pack

Cover sheet, qualifications schedule, programme assumptions, exclusions, and your priced BOQ — issued in your house style, ready to submit.

One-off tenders

Need a single bid priced this week? Send the documents over — we'll quote a fixed fee and turn it round on your submission deadline.
