Deliverable

Submission pack

Cover sheet, qualifications schedule, programme assumptions, exclusions, and your priced BOQ — issued in your house style, ready to submit. The qualifications page wins as many tenders as the price does.

  • Branded to your company identity, not REMC's
  • Qualifications schedule limits risk on access, design changes, programme penalty
  • Submitted 48 hours before deadline where the timeline allows
  • Post-tender query support if the MC comes back with clarifications
Procurement

Sub & material enquiries

Coordinated enquiry packs to your preferred suppliers and subcontractors — sent 48 hours before tender submission so we have time to normalise responses and feed the lowest credible rate into your bid.

  • Sent simultaneously to all subs and merchants with identical scope
  • Tracked in a live enquiry register you can see at any time
  • Responses normalised across suppliers for fair comparison
  • Regional rates — Glasgow pricing for Glasgow tenders
Estimating

Take-off & Bill of Quantities

Bluebeam-marked drawings, audited against the issue revision, translated into a clean, sectioned BOQ you can defend in any post-tender review — containment, first-fix, second-fix, distribution, fire alarm, data, testing, prelims.

  • Bluebeam profile aligned to your house markup style
  • Drawing-by-drawing audit trail issued alongside the bid
  • 30–50% of tender time spent here — foundational to everything else
  • BOQ delivered as native Excel, not a flattened PDF
