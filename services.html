Sector & geographic experience

Pricing the whole UK landscape — Highlands to South Coast.

Typical query response in business hours

REMC's tender book runs across every major UK electrical sector. Residential is the largest single category — housing schemes from West Highland villages through to student accommodation in the South of England, plus refurbishment work across the Lothians and central belt. Hospitality and leisure follows close behind: city-centre hotel and bar fit-outs in Glasgow, Edinburgh and central London; heritage venue restorations; and resort-style refurbishments at major UK leisure parks and racecourses.

Education runs steady on our desk — university estate works across Birmingham, York, Sheffield and London, further-education college refurbishments through the Midlands and Yorkshire, and ASN primary school packages. Healthcare appears regularly, from private hospital fit-outs in Edinburgh and Glasgow through to NHS schemes in the North East. The book also covers retail (Yorkshire and Midlands business parks, high-street units), commercial offices (Glasgow CBD), public sector (police estate, depot rebuilds), logistics and distribution, industrial and manufacturing, and infrastructure works including airport temporary supplies.

Project values range from small fit-out top-ups through to seven-figure heritage installations. If your tender is in any UK electrical sector, anywhere from the Highlands to the South Coast, we've priced one like it recently.
