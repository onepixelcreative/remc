Services

Outsourced electrical estimating — end to end.

From document triage to a qualified, submission-ready bid pack. REMC handles the full preconstruction workflow for UK electrical contractors — Glasgow-based, working with contractors across the United Kingdom.

Service 01

Take-off & Bill of Quantities

Bluebeam-marked drawings, audited against the issue revision, translated into a clean, sectioned BOQ you can defend in any post-tender review. Containment, first-fix, second-fix, distribution, fire alarm, data, specialist, testing, prelims — each item line-by-line with labour, materials, mark-up and prelims as separate columns.

  • Bluebeam profile aligned to your house markup style
  • Drawing-by-drawing audit trail you can issue alongside the bid
  • 30–50% of total tender time spent here — get it right and the rest is straightforward
  • BOQ delivered as native Excel, not a flattened PDF
Service 02

Sub & material enquiries

Coordinated enquiry packs to your preferred suppliers and subcontractors — sent to a deadline 48 hours before tender submission so we have time to normalise responses and feed the lowest credible rate into your bid. Late sub quotes are flagged immediately so you can qualify and submit rather than miss the window.

  • Sent simultaneously to all subs and merchants with identical scope
  • Tracked in a live enquiry register you can see at any time
  • Responses normalised across suppliers for fair comparison
  • Regional rates — Glasgow pricing for Glasgow tenders, London pricing for London
Service 03

Submission pack

Cover sheet, qualifications schedule, programme assumptions, exclusions, and your priced BOQ — issued in your contractor's house style, ready to submit. The qualifications page wins as many tenders as the price does; ours is the clearest part of every submission.

  • Branded to your company identity, not REMC's
  • Qualifications schedule limits risk on access, design changes, pay-when-paid, programme penalty
  • Submitted 48 hours before deadline (where the timeline allows) to leave you time for final review
  • Post-tender query support if the MC comes back with clarifications
Service 04

One-off tenders

Need a single bid priced this week? Send the documents over and we'll quote a fixed fee, accept or decline within 24 hours, and turn the tender around in time for your submission deadline. No retainer required, no commitment beyond the single project.

  • Fixed-fee quoted within 24 hours of brief
  • Pay-on-win pricing — fees sit inside your tender prelims
  • Same delivery standard as our retainer clients
  • If REMC fits, easy to upgrade to a Monthly Service Plan
